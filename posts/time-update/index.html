Time for an update

Posted on Sep 18, 2020
tl;dr: I made a new site, among other things.

Earlier this week I participated as a panelist in a new event series called called Mintor Lounge, which was hosted by my startup Mintor in partnership with Startup Grind - Baton Rouge. The idea behind the event series is to foster a live, exclusive place for intimate conversation and connection among entrepreneurs in the Baton Rouge area.

This inaugural Lounge event was titled “Fearless Self-Branding”, and took the form of a roundtable conversation among the panelists. As we discussed some of the various nuances around self branding, I got to thinking – when was the last time I even looked at my personal site?

So here we are a few days and one brand-spanking-new, minimal (but clean) site later, thanks to Hugo. These days you really can’t beat a quick and easy site that also happens to be auto-generated for you using Markdown. Thanks to zwbetz for the tip! If you’d like to create a similar site, I recommend checking out his article, Make a Hugo blog from scratch.

In times like these, when the majority of our daily interactions with our friends, co-workers, and loved ones are virtual it is more important than ever to make sure you are presenting your best digital self.

This is where I’ll write about software, coding, my experience as a tech startup founder, and some occasional personal musings.

You can also follow me on Twitter as I continue to document my journey.
